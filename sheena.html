Sheena

Full Name: Sheena Mae Catacutan

Stage Name: Sheena, Mamay, Bebe

Birthday: May 9, 2004 (Age 20)

Position in BINI: Main Dancer, Sub-Vocal

Hometown: Sheena is originally from Santiago City, Isabela

Hobbies
Sheena loves dancing and constantly hones her skills by learning new choreography, especially from K-pop songs. She enjoys playing video games as a way to relax and have fun during her downtime. She also likes vlogging and documenting her experiences, which allows her to connect with fans on a personal level.
Inspirations
Sheena is deeply inspired by her family, who have been her biggest supporters since day one. She also looks up to K-pop idols, particularly Lisa of BLACKPINK, for her dance skills, charisma, and determination. Sheena's love for performing and her desire to bring joy to her fans drive her to keep pushing her limits.

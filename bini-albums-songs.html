Da Coconut Nut

BINI's pre-debut single, released on November 20, 2020, paying tribute to Maestro Ryan Cayabyab.

Born to Win

BINI's official debut single, released on June 4, 2021.

Kapit Lang

"Kapit Lang" is BINI's third official single, released on September 10, 2021.

Golden Arrow

"Golden Arrow," BINI's fourth official single, was released on October 14, 2021, at 7:00 PM PHT as the lead single from their first album, "Born To Win."

Na Na Na

Midway through the album, BINI takes a bit of a detour as the next couple of songs revolve around romantic love. At first, “Na Na Na” sounds like a typical romantic song about falling in love. But as you go through the song, it turns into a track about how you keep falling into the same mistakes and ignore the red flags, something a lot of people can relate to.

Kinikilig

This funk-inspired track explores falling in love again when you thought it wasn't possible. While thematically similar, both songs are among the album's best for their musical and vocal quality.

8

"8" is a slower, laid-back jam. While it appears to be about friendship, it also resonates with BINI themselves, as the title refers to the eight members. The repeated phrase "walo hanggang dulo" echoes their journey, highlighting the training and hardships they've faced in pursuit of their goals as a team.

Here With You

The album concludes with "Here With You," an uplifting anthem celebrating the power of friendship and support. This track serves as a heartfelt thank you to BINI's fans, the BLOOMS, making it a fitting final song for concerts.

Pit a Pat

“Pit A Pat” features a blend of English and Filipino lyrics, with the BINI girls contributing to the Filipino sections. Set to tropical beats and a symphonic rhythm, the song conveys an empowering message of resilience in the face of life's challenges.

Lagi

“Lagi” is a vibrant pop song celebrating the excitement of new love with a catchy chorus, written by Louie Canaria and arranged by Ramiru Mataro, featuring vocal production from Jonathan Manalo. It topped the local iTunes chart and was included in Spotify’s New Music Friday Philippines and P-Pop on the Rise playlists.

I Feel Good

BINI's latest bubblegum bop, “I Feel Good,” brings a bright and refreshing vibe, evoking feelings of joy with its playful themes of rainbows and unicorns. The nation's girl group effortlessly captures this cheerful concept, following the success of their previous hits “Na Na Na” and “Lagi.”

No Fear

The song "No Fear" is an empowering anthem about confidence and resilience. The lyrics express a strong determination to overcome challenges and negativity, emphasizing a carefree, positive attitude. Bini is ready to enjoy life and not let anyone stand in their way.

Huwag Muna Tayong Umuwi

“Huwag Muna Tayong Umuwi” by BINI from their sophomore album is also the Official Soundtrack of “An Incovenient Love”.

Strings

"Strings" lyrics focus on overcoming challenges, illustrated by the members—Aiah, Stacey, Maloi, Jhoanna, Gwen, Colet, Sheena, and Mikha—symbolically falling from strings and then transforming to embody strength and determination.

Karera

“Karera” marks a fresh era and sound for the P-pop girl group, delivering an empowering message that encourages listeners to embrace their own pace and not stress over uncontrollable circumstances.

Pantropiko

BINI celebrates young love in their summer-themed single “Pantropiko,” which has garnered over two million views on TikTok. This romantic and upbeat track compares falling in love to a fun summer day.

Salamin, Salamin

“Salamin, Salamin” is a lively track about seeking true love, capturing the excitement of having a crush. It tells the story of a girl dreaming of her prince charming, embodying the joy and anticipation of love.

Ang Huling Cha Cha

"Ang Huling Cha Cha" explores the ups and downs of love, using the metaphor of a dance to depict the emotional rollercoaster of relationships. The lyrics express feelings of confusion and frustration as one partner struggles with the commitment of the other.

Na Na Nandito Lang

"Na Na Nandito Lang" conveys a heartfelt message of support and encouragement during tough times. It reassures someone feeling lost and hurt that they are not alone, emphasizing the importance of resilience and perseverance.

Diyan Ka Lang

"Diyan Ka Lang" expresses the joyful and lighthearted feelings of being in love. It captures the magic of spending time with someone special, where worries and problems fade away in their presence. The lyrics convey a sense of contentment, emphasizing that being with this person is enough; there's no need for anything else.

Cherry On Top

BINI's highly anticipated single "Cherry On Top," released on July 11, 2024, marks a major milestone in their career. The P-Pop sensation collaborated with top-tier songwriters like GG Ramirez, Skylar Mones, Shintaro Yasuda, and Boy Matthews, known for working with stars like Ariana Grande and Dua Lipa.
